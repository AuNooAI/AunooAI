# Deep Dive Template: [Topic] Analysis Framework

## Section 1: Topic Definition & Context Setting
**Template:**
```
[Topic] refers to [clear definition with key characteristics]. 

As there's been [trend indicator - uptick/decline/shift] in articles expressing [sentiment type] about [specific aspect], I thought it a good time for a deep-dive to review the current state of [topic] in the news and reporting.
```

**Example Variables:**
- Topic: AI regulation tightening, AI workplace displacement, AI safety concerns, etc.
- Trend indicator: uptick, decline, notable shift, emerging pattern
- Sentiment type: concerned, optimistic, skeptical, mixed
- Specific aspect: implementation, investment, adoption, policy

## Section 2: Multi-Dimensional Analysis Framework

### 2.1 Primary Analysis Dimensions (Choose 4-5 relevant categories)

**Template Structure:**
```
## [Dimension]: [Descriptor 1], [Descriptor 2], and [Descriptor 3]
Article content
[Dimension]: [Repeat descriptors]
Insight: [2-3 sentence synthesis highlighting key pattern, with specific data points where possible]
```

**Standard Dimension Categories:**
- **Market & Investment:** Funding flows, valuations, investor sentiment
- **Technology:** Capabilities, limitations, development pace, breakthroughs/plateaus
- **Enterprise & Workforce:** Adoption rates, productivity impacts, worker sentiment
- **Regulatory & Policy:** Government actions, compliance costs, legal frameworks
- **Societal & Cultural:** Public opinion, social impacts, cultural shifts
- **Competitive Dynamics:** Market positioning, strategic moves, partnerships
- **Infrastructure & Operations:** Technical requirements, scaling challenges, costs

### 2.2 Insight Formula
**Template:**
```
Insight: [Evidence type] show [stakeholder group] are [action/sentiment], with [specific concern/opportunity] as [specific outcome/metric/trend].
```

## Usage Instructions

1. **Topic Selection:** Choose based on signal strength in vector database and trending categories
2. **Dimension Selection:** Pick 4-5 most relevant dimensions based on available data
3. **Evidence Gathering:** Use sentiment metadata to balance sources across optimistic/skeptical spectrum
4. **Quote Selection:** Prioritize direct quotes that illustrate divergence between narrative and reality
5. **Strategic Insights:** Focus on actionable implications for decision-makers
6. **Length Target:** 800-1200 words total, with 250-300 word conclusion section

This template provides the structural framework while allowing flexibility for topic-specific adaptation based on your vector database results and ontology categories.
