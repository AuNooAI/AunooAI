# Aunoo AI Licensing FAQ

## General Questions

### What license is Aunoo AI under?

Aunoo AI Platform is licensed under the **Business Source License 1.1 (BSL)**, which is a source-available license that converts to open source (Apache 2.0) after 4 years.

### Is Aunoo AI open source?

Aunoo AI is **source-available** now and will become **fully open source** (Apache 2.0) on **November 21, 2028**. Until then, there are some restrictions on commercial use.

### Can I use Aunoo AI for free?

**Yes!** You can use Aunoo AI for free for:
- Internal business use within your organization
- Development, testing, and evaluation
- Academic research and education
- Non-competing commercial products
- Contributing improvements back to the project

- Embed Aunoo AI into your CRM, project management, or other non-competing tools

**Competing Services**
- Offer Aunoo AI as a hosted SaaS platform that competes with Aunoo

- Run Aunoo AI as a multi-tenant service for paying customers

- Creating "YourCompany AI News Platform" powered by Aunoo
- Offering "AI News Analysis as a Service" to third parties
- Hosting Aunoo AI instances for multiple clients as a business

## Comparison with Other Licenses

### BSL vs MIT/Apache (Permissive Open Source)
- **MIT/Apache**: Can use for anything, including competing services
- **BSL**: Can use for anything EXCEPT competing services (for 4 years)

### BSL vs GPL (Copyleft Open Source)
- **GPL**: Must open-source any modifications
- **BSL**: Can keep modifications private, but can't compete

### BSL vs Proprietary
- **Proprietary**: Source code not available
- **BSL**: Source code fully available, just with use restrictions
