## üéØ Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR Article 6, our legal basis for processing your personal data is:

### Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f))
- **Service Provision**: Processing necessary to provide AI analysis and research tools
- **Security**: Fraud prevention and system security
- **Service Improvement**: Analytics to enhance user experience

### Contractual Necessity (Article 6(1)(b))
- **Account Management**: User authentication and access control
- **Service Delivery**: Providing the agreed-upon AI research services

### Consent (Article 6(1)(a))
- **OAuth Authentication**: When you choose to authenticate via Google/GitHub/Microsoft
- **Optional Features**: Enhanced profile features or communications (where applicable)

## üõ°Ô∏è Your GDPR Rights

### Right of Access (Article 15)
- Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- Receive information about how your data is processed
- **How to Exercise**: Email privacy@aunoo.ai with subject "Data Access Request"

### Right to Rectification (Article 16)
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
- Update your profile information at any time
- **How to Exercise**: Log into your account settings or contact privacy@aunoo.ai

### Right to Erasure (Article 17) - "Right to be Forgotten"
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Complete account closure and data removal
- **How to Exercise**: Email privacy@aunoo.ai with subject "Data Deletion Request"

### Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
- Limit how we process your personal data
- Temporary suspension of data processing
- **How to Exercise**: Email privacy@aunoo.ai with specific restriction requests

### Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
- Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- Transfer your data to another service provider
- **How to Exercise**: Email privacy@aunoo.ai with subject "Data Export Request"

### Right to Object (Article 21)
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Opt-out of data processing for specific purposes
- **How to Exercise**: Email privacy@aunoo.ai or adjust account settings

## üïí Data Retention Policy

### Active Users
- **Account Data**: Retained while account is active and for 12 months after last login
- **Chat History**: Retained for 18 months or until user deletion
- **Usage Analytics**: Aggregated data retained for 24 months (anonymized)

### Inactive Users
- **Automatic Deletion**: Accounts inactive for 24+ months are automatically deleted
- **Pre-Deletion Notice**: 30-day notice sent to registered email before deletion
- **Data Backup Purging**: Deleted data removed from all backups within 90 days

### Immediate Deletion
- **User Request**: Data deleted within 30 days of verified deletion request
- **Account Closure**: All personal data removed when account is closed
- **Legal Holds**: Deletion may be delayed if data is subject to legal preservation requirements
